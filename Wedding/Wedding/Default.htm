The Venue

We've reserved the Guemes Island Resort for the whole weekend -- everyone is invited to arrive as early as Thursday afternoon the 21st, and spend the weekend with us!

Travel to the Island

The resort is about two hours north of Seattle, here are driving directions.

Out-of-town guests, please fly in to the Seattle-Tacoma International Airport.

There is very limited parking space at the resort, so please carpool! We also recommend purchasing supplies in Seattle or Anacortes, there's only a small store on the island.

And please note that the last ferry leaves the island at 11pm Friday and Saturday nights.

Accommodations

We've reserved the entire Guemes Island Resort for our wedding, and invite everyone to join us for the long weekend!

The Good News: we're blessed with an abundance of family and friends! :)
The Bad News: the resort itself doesn't have enough housing for everyone. :(

We're going to give out-of-town guests first chance at booking the resort accommodations, and then open what's left to everyone else on a first-come, first-serve basis.

But worry not, there are plenty of other places to crash, and families with kids may choose to avoid the late-night madness and stay a safe distance away. Either way, please check out the other on- and off-island options below, get a group together, and book them before they fill up!

At the Resort

The Guemes Island Resort consists of several charming houses, cabins, and yurts, comfortably housing about a hundred people.



We want as many guests as possible to stay at the resort, so please help us by ensuring the cabins are packed as tightly as possible.

[Updated 2/20: we are finalizing the list of available lodging and setting up the payment system. Around the 3/1, we'll contact out-of-town friends and family to assess interest, and post the remaining cabins and yurts after that.]

On Guemes Island

There are a large number of private residences available for that weekend via AirBnb and VRBO -- please feel free to reach out to them directly.

Make sure to ensure the location is Guemes Island before reserving, and note that there's usually a minimum stay.

Anacortes and Nearby

For those who just want to come for the ceremony and reception and stay over that night, or who would like the luxury of hotel accommodations, there are a number of options in Anacortes.

The last ferry leaves the island at 11pm Friday and Saturday nights.
